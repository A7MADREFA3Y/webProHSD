What is actually flow coding?

When a person sits at a computer and codes with full concentration, losing track of time and feeling effortlessly productive,
this is called being in the flow state (often referred to as the coding flow or programmer’s flow).


What Flow State Means

The flow state, described by psychologist Mihály Csíkszentmihályi, is a mental condition of complete immersion in an activity.
During flow, a person is fully focused, their actions feel natural, and time seems to pass quickly.
